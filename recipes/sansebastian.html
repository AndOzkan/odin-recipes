San Sebastian Cheesecake

Description:

As the name suggests, the San Sebastian Cheesecake, originates from San Sebastian in Spain's Basque Country.

It was a San Sebastian-based restaurant called La Vi√±a that served the San Sebastian cheesecake for the first time.

Ingredients:

  • 5 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 kg cream cheese
  • 1 tbs vanilla extract
  • 1 tbs flour
  • 500 ml double cream
  • 200gr semi sweet chocolate

Directions:

  1. Beat the eggs with the sugar and the vanilla extract
  2. Add the cream cheese to the eggs and sugar mixture then add 300ml of the double cream and finally the flour.
  3. In the oven at 215 degrees Celcius for 25 minutes then at 230 degrees for another 5 minutes
  4. Let it cool for a few hours in the fridge.
  5. Melt the chocolate with the remaining double cream and pour over your cheesecake slice when ready to serve. Enjoy!
