Chocolate Cookies

Description:

These soft-baked thick and chunky double chocolate chip cookies are rich and fudge-like with chewy centers and chocolate chips in each bite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs and 1 yolk
  • 1 cup brown sugar and 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 cups self raising flour and 1/2 cup normal flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tbs vanilla extract
  • 250gr melted butter
  • 200gr milk chocolate and 50gr 70% dark chocolate
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Directions:

  1. Mix flour with the cocoa powder and salt
  2. In a separate bowl whisk the sugar, vanilla extract with the melted butter
  3. When the butter is cool down, add the eggs and whisk together.
  4. Chop the chocolate in rough chunks and leave one chunk per cookie aside for topping later
  5. Mix the dry ingredients with the wet one, including the chocolate chunks until it forms a soft dough then scoop the dough and shape it into a ball the size of a large egg
  6. Place the shapped dough onto a baking tray, press them down and place one chunk on top for each cookie, then bake them in the pre-heated oven at 180C for exactly 8 minutes
